Our Vision



To design a portal where a user will be able to donate, sell, buy or rent books. Due to the current pandemic situation, we will be adding users by providing an option for delivery and pick up. We will be using a library-based model where the books will be collected by the admin and further can be rented or bought by another user. This solves the problem of access to needed books even when shops are closed or printing of books has paused. Furthermore, it helps in solving the issue of a user having books that are not required and hence passing it on to someone who can make better use of it instead of throwing it away. The main advantage of this type of system is that the admin has control over the quality, and authenticity of material procured and hence the customer can remain worry-free when making a decision.
